Can I return the watch, if it doesn't fit?

You can return the watch within 30 days without a reason. Simply send us email to support@aurawatch.com

How can I return/replace my watch?

Recently placed orders can be changed providing they are not yet fulfilled. Please email us at support@aurawatch.com and provide details about the necessary changes.

How long does the delivery take?

Free delivery is within 5-7 business days. For some countries, the delivery period may be different, please consult with the manager from our support team support@aurawatch.com. Express delivery is available for an extra fee.

How much are the shipping costs?

We ship our watches free of charge for all orders over 100 Euros. Please note that if you live outside of the EU or the USA, additional import charges may apply. We recommend checking the import charges for your country before placing an order.

Where do you ship the watches from?

We have three warehouses we can ship from: the Netherlands and Austria for Europe orders, and Hong Kong for the rest of the world.
