Delivery Information

RETURNS

You can return unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. We will also pay the return shipping costs if the return is the result of our error (you received an incorrect or defective item, etc.). You should expect to receive your refund within four weeks of your package being delivered. However, in many cases you will receive a refund more quickly. This time period includes the transit time to receive the return from the shipper (5 to 10 business days), the time it took to process the return once it was received (3 to 5 business days), and the time it took your bank to process our refund request (5 to 10 business days).

SHIPMENT

AuraWatch offers free shipping of all its products to all EU countries which are selectable at the time of shipment. Delivery times range from 1 to 3 days. Delivery times are probable and indicative, but not guaranteed. Deliveries are made from Monday to Friday and include 1 delivery attempt. In case of non-delivery, the courier will deliver a non-delivery notice indicating the number to request delivery within 3 working days. Aura watch offers a free tracking service to provide the buyer with the most efficient and accurate service possible. In case of force majeure, unforeseeable circumstances or third parties for events not attributable to the courier, the delivery will be made at the end of the cause of the delay.
